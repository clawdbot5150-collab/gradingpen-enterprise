Our Story

Transforming Spatial Intelligence

Founded in 2024, GeoSlicing emerged from a vision to democratize advanced geospatial intelligence. Our team of experts in computer science, geography, and data visualization came together to create the most intuitive and powerful spatial analysis platform ever built.

Today, we serve thousands of professionals across industries, from urban planners shaping smart cities to environmental scientists protecting our planet. Our platform processes over 2.8 million data points daily, helping organizations make informed decisions based on spatial insights.

We believe that powerful geospatial tools should be accessible to everyone, not just GIS experts. That's why we've built an platform that combines cutting-edge technology with an intuitive interface that anyone can master.
